BEST SELLER

Cement Stock Bricks

7 MPA strength cement bricks. Perfect for housing walls and extensions.

R11 each (delivered)*
  • Size: 220mm x 110mm x 70mm
  • 7 MPA compressive strength
  • ~55 bricks per m² (single)
  • Minimum order: 500 bricks
  • Truck capacity: 5000 bricks

Maxi Bricks (RDP)

Double-volume bricks. Save on cement and labor with larger size.

R3.50 each (ex-factory)
  • Size: 290mm x 140mm x 90mm
  • Single skin construction
  • ~35 bricks per m²
  • 30% cost saving per m²
  • Delivery: R500 per load

PPC Cement

32.5R and 42.5N cement for mixing and plastering. Delivered in full truck loads.

R85 per 50kg bag*
  • PPC Surebuild or equivalent
  • 32.5R for general use
  • 42.5N for structural
  • ~3 bags per 1000 bricks
  • Truck carries 500 bags
